Our Services

Sunday Worship

We gather together every Sunday morning at 10:45am to worship together through music, the proclamation of the Word and observation of the ordinances.

Discipleship

Before our weekly worship service on Sunday we have classes studying through the Word, praying for one another starting at 9:30am.

Outreach

We regularly conduct outreach ministries to communities throughout Utah, sharing the Gospel of Jesus Christ.

Community Groups

Small groups of members meet Sunday evenings to hold one another accountable and discuss the subject of the Sunday sermon.
